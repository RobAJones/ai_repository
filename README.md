# DFRobot 1154 AI Camera 
The DFRobot 1154 AI camera representa a low cost ESP32S3 Camera based solution to integrating AI into your projects.
## The DFRoto 1154 AI camera has video with IR illumination, audio (microphone and audio amplifier with speaker), combined with 16MB of flash to offer a well integrated platform.

## Hardware

-ESP-S3R8 Dual-Core 240MHz, 8MB PSRAM
-OV3660 160 Degree wide-angle, infrared capabble camera with IR illumination
-PDM Microphone Built-in I2S digital mic (GPIO 38/39)
-MAX98357 Audio Amp I2S output for TTS audio
-Micro SS Slot  upto 32GB memory storage
-16MB Flash
-VIn 3.7-15V
