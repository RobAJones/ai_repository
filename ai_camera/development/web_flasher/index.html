AI Camera

Flash the firmware to your ESP32-S3 AI CAM directly from your browser. No software to install. Just plug in, click, and go.

Plug in your ESP32-S3 via USB-C, then click the button below.

Your browser doesn't support Web Serial.
Use Chrome or Edge on desktop. Web Serial is not allowed in this context.
Make sure you're accessing this page over HTTPS.

Before You Start

  • Chrome or Edge browser (desktop only)
  • USB-C data cable (not charge-only)
  • ESP32-S3 AI CAM (DFRobot DFR1154)
  • Micro SD card inserted in the board

After Flashing

  1. The camera will create a WiFi network called AI-Camera-Setup
  2. Connect to it from your phone or laptop (no password needed)
  3. A setup page will open automatically. Pick your WiFi network and enter your password.
  4. Paste your OpenAI API key (get one at platform.openai.com/api-keys)
  5. Click Connect & Start Camera. The camera will reboot and you're done!
  6. Open the IP address shown on the setup page to use the camera interface.

Tips & Tricks

💡 Not all USB cables are the same. If your board isn't showing up, swap cables. Charging cables look identical but can't transfer data.
🔌 The BOOT button is your best friend. Hold it while clicking Flash if things get stuck. Hold it for 3 seconds to factory reset.
📡 Same WiFi, same network. Your phone/laptop and the camera need to be on the same WiFi to talk to each other.
🤖 Ask it anything. Take a photo of your homework, a bug in the garden, or your lunch. The AI sees what the camera sees.
💰 API costs are tiny. Each request costs about 1-2 cents. Set a spending limit in your OpenAI dashboard to stay safe.
🧪 Experiment! Send multiple photos at once, combine audio + images, try different prompts. There's no wrong way to explore.

Troubleshooting

  1. No device detected? Try a different USB cable. Some cables are charge-only and can't transfer data.
  2. Flash failed? Hold the BOOT button on the board while clicking Flash, then release after it starts.
  3. Need to start over? Hold the BOOT button for 3 seconds to factory reset and re-enter setup.
