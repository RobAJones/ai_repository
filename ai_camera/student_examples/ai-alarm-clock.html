AI Alarm Clock

A student project using the AI Camera API

How to Use

  1. Enter your AI Camera's IP address (find it on the camera's web interface)
  2. Click "Test Connection" to make sure it works
  3. Set your alarm time and click "Set Alarm"
  4. When the alarm goes off, it will take your photo, listen for your voice, and give you a personalized wake-up message!
